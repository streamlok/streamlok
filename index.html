How It Started ?

We found that there were thousands of excellent web series available, every one offering amazing storytelling twists, stories, and direction. However, we were extra careful to watch these programmes with our families and suggest them to others because of the explicit and sexual scenes. We acknowledged that this was a problem for a lot of people, particularly those from mixed families who had little privacy and few resources, as well as those who had cultural restrictions on seeing explicit material. The necessity for a platform that could offer a censored version of these web series became apparent to us at that point, and we made the decision to found a business that would provide this service. Streamlok was created at that time. 3 - who are started this company This company was established by three individuals, of which two are software engineers and the third is a student with a Bachelor of Arts degree.
